Chicken Puff Pastries

Home

Description

This is an easy to make recipe using pre-made dough and other easily sourced ingredients.

The result is a healthy and delicious meal made in less than 45 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Garlic
  • Cream Cheese (One Block)
  • Bag of Italian Cheese
  • Spinach
  • Muffin Tin

Steps

  1. Cook chicken to your preference.
  2. Heat cream cheese in large microwavable bowl (approx 30s).
  3. Once cream cheese is heated, mix in remaining ingrediants including chicken.
  4. Once ingredients are thoroughly mixed, place puff pastries in muffin tin.
  5. Fill puff pastries with mixed ingredients, then cover with remaining dough.
  6. Cook to istructions on pastry packaging.
