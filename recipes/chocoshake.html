Chocolate, PB, Banana Protein Shake

Home

Description

This is an easy and delicious meal to make after a workout.

The result is a quick and nutritious drink in under 15 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Chocolate Protein Powder
  • Banana
  • Peanut Butter
  • Milk
  • Ice
  • Blender

Steps

  1. Pour milk into blender (approx. 1/4 cup).
  2. Scoop protein powder into blender (30G recommended).
  3. Squeeze banana into blender.
  4. Add ice until full to blender line.
  5. Cover lid and blend until the drink is a smooth consistency.
  6. Enjoy!
